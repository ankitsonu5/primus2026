Lifespan

  • Total number of years lived
  • Often extended by medical intervention
  • Later years may include illness or dependency
  • Focused on survival

Healthspan

  • Years lived in good health
  • Built through prevention, movement, and nutrition
  • Independence, energy, and joy for longer
  • Focused on living well
At Primus, we design life to extend Healthspan – so more years are truly lived.

What is Active Ageing?

Active ageing is about staying physically mobile, mentally engaged, and socially connected

Move Daily

Gentle, regular movement keeps the body strong, balanced, and independent.

Engage the Mind

Learning, hobbies, and conversations keep curiosity alive.

Stay Connected

Community, friendships, and shared experiences build emotional wellbeing.

Live with Purpose

Meaningful routines and contributions give every day direction.

At Primus, active ageing is built into everyday life - through design, routines, and community.
