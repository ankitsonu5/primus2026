Think ageing can't be reversed? Think again.

Healthspan vs Lifespan

Lifespan

  • Total number of years lived
  • Often extended by medical intervention
  • Later years may include illness or dependency
  • Focused on survival

Healthspan

  • Years lived in good health
  • Built through prevention, movement, and nutrition
  • Independence, energy, and joy for longer
  • Focused on living well
At Primus, we design life to extend Healthspan – so more years are truly lived.
