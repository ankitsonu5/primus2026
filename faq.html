FAQs

1. What makes Primus one of the best luxury senior living communities in India?
Primus is recognised for its luxury senior living homes with senior-friendly design, a vibrant community life, 24x7 healthcare access, curated activities, and comprehensive support services that make retirement fulfilling and socially engaging.
2. Where are Primus senior citizen homes available?
Primus has established senior living communities in major cities like Bangalore, Kolkata, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune and Chennai, each offering safe, well-connected environments with essential services and amenities for seniors.
3. What types of homes are offered at Primus Darpan in Bangalore?
At Primus Darpan, seniors can choose from thoughtfully designed apartments (1, 2, 2.5 BHK) with accessibility features, beautiful landscaping, a restaurant, medical facilities, clubhouse, pool, and community amenities tailored for comfortable post-retirement living.
4. What services are included in Primus retirement homes in Bangalore and Chennai?
Primus communities provide:
  • 24/7 healthcare coordination and emergency support
  • Housekeeping and maintenance
  • Concierge services
  • Nutritious dining and chef-prepared meals
  • Social events, fitness and wellness programs
  • Barrier-free design for safety and mobility
These services ensure day-to-day ease and peace of mind.
5. What amenities can residents enjoy at Primus senior living communities?
Residents enjoy a comprehensive range of amenities including:
  • Landscaped outdoor spaces
  • Fitness and wellness zones
  • Clubhouse and activity centers
  • Swimming pool
  • Dining & restaurant facilities
  • Social and event spaces
These are designed to nurture health, connection, and active lifestyles.
6. Do Primus senior homes support multigenerational or family living?
Yes, some Primus multigenerational living communities allow families to live together while maintaining independence, with shared spaces and services that support multiple age groups.
7. What is the lifestyle philosophy behind Primus senior living?
Primus emphasises a holistic lifestyle that integrates comfort, community engagement, purposeful activities, and wellness services so seniors can lead fulfilling lives rather than just "age-in-care".
8. What makes the senior citizen homes in Bangalore unique?
Primus homes in Bangalore are strategically located near healthcare, cultural hubs, parks, and conveniences, blending serene environments with vibrant urban access, ideal for senior homes that promote active and secure living.

Many Primus communities support intergenerational living, offering larger apartments and shared spaces where family members can stay close while preserving independence.
