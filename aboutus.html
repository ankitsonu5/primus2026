At Primus, we believe reimagining life begins at home. Homes where children learn from grandparents, parents stay close yet independent, and everyday living is filled with laughter, support, and togetherness.

Independence and interdependence, designed to co-exist beautifully.

How it all began

It started with our founder Adarsh Narahari’s ajji. She wanted independence with dignity — and inspired Adarsh to study the best senior living communities worldwide. In 2013, that vision became Primus: Indian homes where community and freedom thrive side by side.

Led by a Pioneer.

Adarsh Narahari

A pioneer in building senior communities in India that focus not just on living longer, but living stronger, healthier, and happier.

His work blends Blue Zone principles, preventive wellness, and thoughtful environments that enable movement, nutrition, purpose, and connection.

Through Primus, he champions a simple but transformative belief: healthspan should be greater than lifespan.

Hemant Taneja
One of America's most respected entrepreneur and is Managing Director at General Catalyst. GC is a $47Bn US-based fund that specializes in healthcare and also runs health at the world’s largest senior living community, The Villages, which is a 35,000-acre retirement destination
Nikhil Kamath
One of India's most respected entrepreneurs, known for backing companies that improve lives in meaningful, measurable ways.

With a philosophy rooted in purposeful investing, Nikhil champions transformative ideas that deepen access, elevate quality of life, and create long-term value for communities.

His belief in building businesses that do good, not just do well, aligns strongly with the mission of Primus.

Backed by Visionaries.
